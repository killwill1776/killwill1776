Born in the Machine

About

KillWill is the byproduct of a lifelong musician who grew up inside the machine.

Born into a deeply musical family and raised around the industry's inner circle, he learned the craft early. Music wasn't a career he chose — it was the air he grew up breathing. By the time most people are figuring out what they want to do, he'd already spent years in the rooms most never see: national television, major-label sessions, and world tours alongside some of the biggest names in music.

He did the work. He played the game. He earned his place in it.

Then something shifted. Watching the division spreading everywhere — much of it driven by forces the public never sees — he found his niche and channeled that fire into his lyrics.

Years of production and songwriting had taught him how to let songs write themselves. What poured out was unfiltered: patriotism meets punk — genre-bending outlaw hip-hop laced with rock, soul, and country, written and produced independently with no label and no filter.

He's one artist speaking with boldness and conviction — telling the truth as he sees it, and turning it into songs that hit with principle and purpose.
